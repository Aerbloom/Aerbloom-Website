Signature flavor

Basil

The taste of freshly picked basil leaves — green, luminous, citrus-bright, and unmistakably refined.

Aerbloom Basil was developed to avoid the cooked, anise-like profile often found in basil oils and instead preserve the raw, herbal brightness of fresh leaves. Cold maceration in vegetable glycerin helps retain delicate green and floral compounds, while a standardized natural extract supports consistency from batch to batch. The result feels like opening a fresh garden: pure, vivid, and alive.

Taste architecture

What defines Basil

Basil is the clearest statement of the Aerbloom philosophy: natural sophistication, balance, and sensory freshness without heaviness.

  • Raw green basil character
  • Floral lift
  • Bright citrus acidity
  • Light sweetness from pure cane sugar

Serve moments

How it shines

Basil is designed to work as both a complete non-alcoholic drink and a hospitality-ready mixer with clarity and restraint.

  • Served chilled on its own
  • Paired with gin
  • Poured as an aperitif
  • Used in garden-forward mocktails

Flavor notes

Basil in four cues

Fresh-picked basil

The profile opens with the sensation of torn basil leaf rather than syrup, oil, or cooked herb.

Green luminosity

Its freshness is bright and plant-forward, with the airy quality of a garden rather than a pantry spice.

Citrus structure

Acidity gives the flavor lift and keeps the herbal notes clean, focused, and refreshing.

Polished finish

The finish stays composed and sparkling, with depth but no heaviness.
