Basil

A vivid green botanical profile with fresh basil character, refined citrus lift, and a clean sparkling finish.

Basil is the anchor of the Aerbloom collection: herbaceous, elegant, and deliberately upscale. It is designed to feel equally at home on a retail shelf, in a chilled minibar, or poured in stemware as a zero-proof aperitif.

Taste architecture

What defines Basil

Basil is intended to feel garden-fresh and composed. The profile is built to read premium from the first sip, with enough character to stand alone and enough restraint to pair well with food and hospitality settings.

  • Fresh basil leaf
  • Soft citrus brightness
  • Deep green aromatics
  • Dry, polished finish

Serve moments

Where it belongs

Basil is especially suited for premium moments where conventional soda would feel too casual and sparkling water would feel too minimal.

  • Mediterranean plates
  • Seafood and crudo
  • Aperitif service
  • Garden-forward mocktails
