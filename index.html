Brand point of view

Built for premium retail and modern non-alcoholic occasions.

Aerbloom’s aesthetic sits between botanical elegance and contemporary hospitality. The green palette, floral textures, and single-line emblem give the brand a shelf presence that feels more like fragrance, tea, or aperitif than conventional soda.

That makes this site useful beyond direct-to-consumer selling. It can introduce the range to bar managers, hotel buyers, retailers, collaborators, and early customers in a way that already feels elevated.

FAQ

Good next moves from here

Can this site work right away without a framework?

Yes. It is plain HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and image assets, so you can upload it directly to a static host or to hosting attached to your domain.

Can this later become a real online store?

Yes. The layout and page structure are ready to port into Shopify, WordPress, or a custom storefront when you are ready for payments, inventory, and fulfillment.

Can I connect this to my GoDaddy domain?

Yes. Keep the domain registered at GoDaddy and point the DNS to Netlify, Cloudflare Pages, GitHub Pages, or a standard hosting account.

What should be customized next?

Your real order flow, contact email, price points, ingredients, nutrition facts, stockist page, and final launch copy.
