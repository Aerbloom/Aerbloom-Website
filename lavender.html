Lavender

A floral-led expression made to feel serene, elevated, and especially giftable in premium hospitality settings.

Lavender brings the collection into a softer register. It is not meant to read perfumed or overly sweet; the goal is a composed floral finish with visual softness and a premium ritual feel.

Taste architecture

What defines Lavender

Lavender is intended to feel calm, floral, and giftable. The profile is built to read premium from the first sip, with enough character to stand alone and enough restraint to pair well with food and hospitality settings.

  • Aromatic floral lift
  • Soft herbal undertone
  • Calm, airy palate
  • Delicate dry finish

Serve moments

Where it belongs

Lavender is especially suited for premium moments where conventional soda would feel too casual and sparkling water would feel too minimal.

  • Tea service
  • Spa and wellness venues
  • Dessert menus
  • Afternoon sipping
