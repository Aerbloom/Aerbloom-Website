Lavender

Floral, soft, and soothing, with a calm, meditative finish that feels airy and elegant.

Lavender is the most serene expression in the Aerbloom line. It is designed to feel gentle and atmospheric, with floral softness carried by a clean sparkling frame. In the collection, Lavender represents calmness and stillness — a flavor meant for quiet pleasure, graceful hospitality, and moments when refreshment should feel almost weightless.

Taste architecture

What defines Lavender

Lavender is intentionally restrained so the floral character stays graceful, fresh, and sophisticated rather than perfumed.

  • Soft floral aromatics
  • Airy botanical sweetness
  • Gentle sparkling lift
  • Soothing composed finish

Serve moments

How it shines

Lavender is built for the most delicate side of the brand: slow rituals, evening pauses, and hospitality moments that reward subtlety.

  • Served chilled on its own
  • Paired with gin or white rum
  • Used in zero-proof spritzes
  • Poured in calm evening service

Flavor notes

Lavender in four cues

Floral softness

The first impression is airy and gentle, designed to soothe rather than overwhelm.

Quiet sweetness

Any sweetness is kept measured so the flavor remains elegant and adult.

Pastel mood

Lavender carries the collection’s calmest emotional register: poetic, still, and refined.

Clean finish

The aftertaste is delicate, sparkling, and controlled.

Occasions

Built for ritual, menu placement, and shelf presence.

Wind-down moments

A graceful option for late afternoon and evening when something gentle feels right.

Hotel and minibar service

Its mood and design fit beautifully into restful hospitality environments.

Refined gifting

Lavender adds softness and serenity to the overall collection.

Part of a system

Lavender belongs to a wider botanical garden.

Every Aerbloom flavor shares the same brand philosophy: natural sophistication, balanced refreshment, and a can design that feels more like an object of taste than a conventional soft drink. Together, the line is designed for bars, hotels, premium retail, and everyday rituals of mindful refreshment.
