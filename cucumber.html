Cucumber

Crystal-clear, pure, and aquatic, with a cooling finish that feels weightless and bright.

Cucumber brings purity and clarity into the Aerbloom world. It is the line’s cleanest expression: refreshing, spa-like, and transparent in mood. Rather than chasing sweetness or intensity, Cucumber focuses on precision and lightness — the feeling of chilled water, fresh air, and calm refreshment translated into a more expressive sparkling format.

Taste architecture

What defines Cucumber

Cucumber is designed to feel effortless and exceptionally clean — a flavor that refreshes immediately without ever feeling empty.

  • Fresh cucumber clarity
  • Aquatic lightness
  • Cooling lift
  • Polished sparkling finish

Serve moments

How it shines

Cucumber performs best wherever calm, refreshment, and elegance matter more than intensity: daytime service, wellness-adjacent hospitality, and clean minimalist drinking rituals.

  • Served extra cold on its own
  • Paired with vodka
  • Used in spa-like spritzes
  • Featured in daytime hospitality
