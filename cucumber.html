Taste architecture

What defines Cucumber

Cucumber is intended to feel cooling and refined. The profile is built to read premium from the first sip, with enough character to stand alone and enough restraint to pair well with food and hospitality settings.

  • Cool cucumber freshness
  • Crisp herbal lift
  • Clean palate
  • Spa-like finish

Serve moments

Where it belongs

Cucumber is especially suited for premium moments where conventional soda would feel too casual and sparkling water would feel too minimal.

  • Salads and light lunches
  • Hotels and spas
  • Daytime occasions
  • Low-intervention mocktails
