Jalapeño

Green, tangy, and lightly spicy, with a vivid sparkle that feels bright rather than heavy.

Jalapeño brings brightness and energy into the Aerbloom garden. It is designed to feel green and lively, with a clean tang and a restrained heat that wakes up the palate without overwhelming it. In the collection it carries the emotional register of brightness — a more extroverted flavor that still stays polished, natural, and elegant.

Taste architecture

What defines Jalapeño

Jalapeño is calibrated to keep the spice fresh and modern — expressive enough to feel exciting, but clean enough to remain versatile.

  • Fresh green pepper note
  • Lively tang
  • Light botanical heat
  • Sparkling clean finish

Serve moments

How it shines

Jalapeño is especially useful when the moment calls for freshness and attitude rather than sweetness or heaviness.

  • Served ice-cold on its own
  • Paired with tequila blanco
  • Poured in spicy highballs
  • Used in vibrant mocktails

Flavor notes

Jalapeño in four cues

Green freshness

The pepper note feels vegetal and alive rather than smoky or cooked.

Tangy attack

Acidity gives the flavor immediate brightness and a lifted first sip.

Measured spice

The heat is controlled and elegant, adding energy instead of aggression.

Modern finish

The profile ends crisp and sparkling, leaving clarity rather than burn.

Occasions

Built for ritual, menu placement, and shelf presence.

Sunny daytime service

A bright choice for patios, beach menus, and warm-weather gatherings.

Tequila-friendly menus

It slots naturally into tequila-driven programs and modern agave service.

Zero-proof excitement

A strong option for guests who want movement and intensity without alcohol.
