Jalapeño

A bright botanical soda with green pepper character and a clean, contemporary kick rather than heavy sweetness.

Jalapeño gives the range a sharper edge. The intention is not aggressive heat; it is freshness, lift, and a modern food-and-beverage profile that stands out in retail and on menus.

Taste architecture

What defines Jalapeño

Jalapeño is intended to feel energetic and contemporary. The profile is built to read premium from the first sip, with enough character to stand alone and enough restraint to pair well with food and hospitality settings.

  • Fresh green pepper
  • Lime-like brightness
  • Light botanical warmth
  • Snappy sparkling finish

Serve moments

Where it belongs

Jalapeño is especially suited for premium moments where conventional soda would feel too casual and sparkling water would feel too minimal.

  • Tacos and ceviche
  • Poolside menus
  • Spicy food pairings
  • Modern non-alcoholic cocktails
